Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

3SXREMIT is a remittance service that allows users to send money securely and efficiently to various destinations worldwide.

To create an account, click on the "Create Account" link in the navigation bar and fill out the registration form with your personal details.

You can contact customer support by visiting our "Contact Us" page and filling out the contact form or by calling our support hotline.

The time it takes for the money to be received varies by destination and payment method. Typically, transactions are completed within 1-3 business days or even seconds.
